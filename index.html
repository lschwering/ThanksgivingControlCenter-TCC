Slow Cooker BBQ Ribs
7h LOW + 15‚Äì20 min broil

Prep

  • Remove membrane from back of ribs.
  • Season heavily with dry rub (salt, pepper, paprika, garlic powder, brown sugar, chili powder).
  • Optional: add ~1/4 cup apple juice or broth to slow cooker for moisture.

  • Place ribs standing or curled around inside of slow cooker.
  • Cook 7 hours on LOW until very tender.

  • Transfer ribs to a foil-lined sheet pan.
  • Brush generously with BBQ sauce.
  • Broil on HIGH 15‚Äì20 minutes until caramelized and a bit charred on edges.

  • Rest 5‚Äì10 minutes so they don‚Äôt fall apart too fast.

Best Mashed Potatoes
Creamy, buttery, no fuss

Core Ratio

  • ~1.6 lb potatoes (russet or Yukon), garlic optional.
  • Butter: be generous; ~0.13‚Äì0.25 cup melted.
  • Warm milk/cream: ~0.4 cup, added gradually.

  • Drain very well, let steam off, then mash by hand.
  • Add melted butter first, then warm cream until fluffy.
  • Season with salt & pepper. Hold warm or reheat, dotted with butter.
